Smilenation

Smilenation Child Support Initiative is a registered non-profit organization operating under the Incorporated Trustees framework with Registration Number CAC/IT/No.172370. Established to address the pressing needs of underserved children, our mission centers on fostering access to quality education, promoting vocational skill acquisition, and empowering young minds to become future nation-builders.

Our Objectives

At Smilenation Child Support Initiative, we work diligently to support less-privileged children by facilitating their education both inside and outside the classroom. Our holistic approach focuses on grooming these young individuals with essential vocational skills, fostering self-reliance, and positioning them for impactful roles in nation-building. By doing so, we aim to transform lives, positively impacting communities and laying a strong foundation for societal progress. We envision a world where every child, regardless of their background, has access to the tools, knowledge, and opportunities to succeed and become active contributors to society.

Our History and Evolution

The organization was officially established in March 2019 as Smilenation Foundation, with the primary goal of promoting quality education and equipping young talents with the necessary tools to thrive on a global scale. Recognizing the need for a more comprehensive approach, we underwent rebranding in August 2022, emerging as Smilenation Child Support Initiative to better reflect our broadened scope and refined objectives.
